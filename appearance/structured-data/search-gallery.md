# Structured data markup that Google Search supports

Google uses [structured data](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/intro-structured-data) to understand the content on the page and show that content in a richer appearance in search
 results, which is called a *rich result*. To make your site eligible for appearance as
 one of these rich results, follow the guide to learn how to implement structured data on your site.
 If you're just getting started, visit [Understand how structured data works](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/intro-structured-data).

> [!NOTE]
> **Note**
>
> : The actual appearance in search results might be different. You can

>

Structured data features **Article** A news, sports, or blog article displayed in various rich result features, such as
 the title of the article and larger-than-thumbnail images. [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/article)![article example in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/article-rich-result.png) **Breadcrumb** Navigation that indicates the page's position in the site hierarchy. [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/breadcrumb)![breadcrumb example in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/breadcrumb.png) **Carousel** Rich results that display in a sequential list or gallery from a single site. This
 feature must be combined with one of the following features: [Recipe](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/recipe), [Course list](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/course), [Restaurant](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/local-business#carousel), [Movie](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/movie). [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/carousel)![An illustration of how a recipe host carousel can appear in Google Search. It shows 3 different recipes from the same website in a carousel format that users can explore and select a specific recipe](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/recipe-host-carousel-rich-result.png) **Course list** A list of educational course from the same course provider. Courses can include
 the course title, provider, and a short description. [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/course)![course list rich result in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/course-carousel-rich-result.png) **Dataset** Large data sets that appear in Google Dataset Search. [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/dataset)![dataset example in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/dataset-search.png) **Discussion forum** User-generated content (traditionally short-form compared to `Article`),
 followed by a threaded or non-threaded discussion about that topic. [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/discussion-forum)![An illustration of the discussions and forums rich result](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/discussions-and-forums-rich-result.png) **Education Q&A** Education-related questions and answers that help students discover flashcards on Google Search. [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/education-qa)![Education Q&A carousel in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/education-qa-rich-result.png) **Employer aggregate rating** An evaluation of a hiring organization compiled from many users that's displayed in
 the job search experience on Google. [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/employer-rating)![employer aggregate rating example in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/employer-aggregate-rating01.png) **Event** An interactive rich result that shows a list of organized events, such as concerts
 or art festivals, that people may attend at a particular time and place. [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/event)![How the event experience looks on Google Search](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/event-rich-result.png) **Image metadata** When you specify image metadata, Google Images can show more details about the image,
 such as who the creator is, how people can use an image, and credit information. [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/image-license-metadata)![An example of Image Metadata in Google Images](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/image-metadata.png) **Job posting** An interactive rich result that allows job seekers to find a job. The job search

[Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/job-posting)![job posting example in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/jobs-search-ui.png) **Local business** Business details displayed in the Google knowledge panel, including open hours,

[Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/local-business)![local business example in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/local-business02.png) **Math solver** Help students, teachers, and others with math problems by adding structured data to

[Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/math-solvers)![math solvers in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/math-solvers-rich-result.png) **Movie** The movie carousel helps users explore lists of movies on Google Search (for example,

 each movie, director information, and images. [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/movie)![An illustration of how a movie rich result can appear in Google Search. It shows 3 different movies from the same website in a carousel format that users can explore and select a specific movie](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/movie-rich-result.png) **Organization** Information about your organization, such as your logo, legal name of the

[Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/organization)![organization example in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/organization.png) **Product** Information about a product, including price, availability, and review ratings. [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/product)![product example in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/search-gallery-products.png) **Profile page** A page that primarily focuses on information about a single person or organization

[Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/profile-page)![An illustration of the Perspectives filter in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/discussions-and-forums-rich-result.png) **Q&A** Q&A Pages are web pages that contain data in a question and answer format,

[Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/qapage)![question answer page example in search results](https://developers.google.com/static/search/docs/images/qa-example-screenshot.png) **Recipe** Recipes that display as an individual rich result or part of a host carousel. [Get started](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/recipe)![An illustration of how a recipe host carousel can appear in Google Search. A review snippet can be about [Book](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/book), [Recipe](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/recipe), [Movie](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/movie), [Product](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/product), [Software App](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/software-app),
